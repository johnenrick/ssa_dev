Digital Microprocessor Laboratory

    The Digital and Microprocessor Laboratory was established in 1990's as one of he section under EE/ECE/ComE Department, which was named then as Digital Laboratory. In 1995, The Computer Engineering Department was separated from the department where DML was officially under it. There was about 5 different faculty designated as section heads since start of the operation of the laboratory. The section head directly reports to the chairman of the department.

Mission

 
The hardware cluster of the computer engineering department aims to address the following:
-
Meet with hardware cluster members once a month to discuss matters pertaining to the development of the laboratory in its facilities and equipment as well as the services rendered to the clients
-
The Faculty member should continually attend seminars, workshops, symposiums, forums, and other related activities to keep abreast of the technical development in their areas
-
Provide a separate research development laboratory for faculty interested in research
-
Equip the laboratory with hardware development facilities and software tools for simulation and emulation on the microprocessor-based design and implementation
-
Cater clients from outside the institution for consultancy
-
Conduct seminars and workshops catering other institutions and industry as well
 
 

Vision

To become the center of development and excellence in the field of Computer Hardware, Digital Systems, Data Acquisition, Embedded Systems, and Very Large Scale Integration(VLSI) in its analysis, design, and realization which challenge the need of our clients.
To become an effective and competent researcher in this field
